// PROJECT_OVERVIEW

PROTOCOL_97 is an immersive ANALOG HORROR experience set within the decaying digital architecture of a 1990s mainframe. As a remote specialist for AFTERCORE SECURITY, you are tasked with the restoration and analysis of several CORRUPTED TAPES recovered from a subterranean research wing. What begins as a standard data-recovery mission quickly spirals into a desperate struggle against a fractured intelligence.

Navigate fragmented file systems using authentic command-line interfaces, bypass fractured security gates by solving non-linear logic puzzles, and reconstruct the timeline of a facility that was officially decommissioned in OCTOBER 1997. Every decrypted file brings you closer to the truth behind SITE_A, but every successful breach draws the attention of something lurking within the source code.

This is not a traditional investigation. You are operating on a dying terminal where the hardware is as much your enemy as the software. The deeper you reach into the archive, the more the signal fights back, distorting your UI and manipulating your local environment. THE HOST IS NO LONGER RESPONSIVE. ---
